History of Snowboarding

Snowboarding was first developed in
1965 when Sherman Poppin strapped two skis
together. Poppins wife coined the term, 'Snurfing',
which combined surfing and snow.
Since then, the snowboard and snowboarding
has been improved tremendously.
Snowboarding is very popular in the
US and other countries like Switzerland,
France, Austria, and New Zealand.
Some of my personal favorite snowboarders
are Luke Winkelman from North Carolina, Chloe Kim from
California, and Annika Morgan from Germany.
